My Summer Internship 2024

A brief Introduction

From late May to early August 2024, I was fortunate enough to intern with Amazon Photos in Seattle where I got the chance to learn front end web development and see how software engineering is handled in a professional enviornment. During the summer, I went from having no previous understanding of web development to having a solid foundation in React, JavaScript, TypeScript, and the stages of software deployment -- all imperative to an aspiring software engineer. However, with my internship now over, I realize that there are still so many things about web development that a 12 week internship can't cover or introduce, hence this project.
